Notebooks

Notebooks keep you organized. Think of a notebook like a binder that has all of your Notes inside.

To create a new Notebook you simply click the "New" button on the right of where it says Notebooks

Save Web Clippings

See a website or article you like? Copy and paste the website to create a Note. Just a quick clean up and alignment and its yours forever.

To create a new Note within your Notebook you click on the "+" sign beside your Notebook
