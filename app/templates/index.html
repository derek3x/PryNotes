Organize

Keep your notes organized in file cabinets and notebooks. Keep your life or business organized and stream lined for maximum efficiency.

Encrypted

It's your data. Keep it safe and secure. We encrypt all note bodies with 256 bit encryption. We don't want to know what you have in your notes, and believe others should not either.

Access Anywhere

Keeping your notes, ideas, and interests stored within your notes allows you to access your data from anywhere. This will protect you from losing data or devices that contain your important information. Access your data where ever you go and when ever you want.

Share Notes

Share your notes with a shared URL link to anyone. No PryNotes account is needed to see the notes you have shared with someone.

Privacy

We will always respect your privacy. We do not go through your notes for ads, sell your data to advertising companies, or keep track of you personally. You can read more in our help section about the privacy steps we take.
