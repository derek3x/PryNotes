Help Items:

What are File Cabinets?

File Cabinets make it possible to organize your notebooks. For example, you can have a fitness File Cabinet, then have different Notebooks for different fitness topics. You could have one Notebook for workouts, diets, and articles. Then have Notes in each Notebook so it is all organized and easily found when you need the information.

What are Notebooks?

Notebooks are there so you can keep a collection of Notes in an organized and easy to find location. For example, you could have a Notebook about BBQ Recipies. Then inside that Notebook have a bunch of different Notes that are BBQ Recipes.

Are the Notes Encrypted?

Yes, we encrypt the body of your Notes. This means we do not encrypt your File Cabinet, Notebook, or Note Titles. The body of every Note is encrypted with 256 bit encryption. We do not suggest saving your passwords or important confidential information such as bank accounts or credit card numbers.

How do I Create a File Cabinet?

You simply click the New button on the right of the File Cabinets

How do I Create a Notebook?

You simply click the New button on the right of the Notebooks

How do I Create a Note?

You simply click the button on the right of the Notebooks

Is my Information Safe?

We have done our best to make your information safe. We will not mine your data for ads, we will not sell your data, and we encrypt your notes so even if we wanted to, we can't see your notes.

I Forgot my Password

We can not help you. The login is done through a third party (google, yahoo, etc). If you lose your password with them you will have to reset it with them.

Why Free?

We all like a free service. This site will be free as long as we can keep it that way. There will soon be a donation button if you want to help with the server costs and pay the developer.

Can I Change my Nickname?

Yes, click on the settings near the top right of the browser window. You can make your name what ever you wish.

Do you track us?

No. We only get your nickname(username), and email address with your account you used to sign in with. We keep track of your last visit so we can delete old accounts if need be in the future. For website analytics we parse and strip our own logs. So bluntly, we don't want to track you and never will. This site was created due to privacy concerns with other websites, so we will not create the same hostile environment.

Can I Delete my Account?

Yes, you can delete your account at anytime from the settings page. If you delete your account we will delete all of your notes, notebooks, and filecabinets. Your user account will be purged from the database and nothing will be saved or be possibly retrieved after deletion.

    What Is a Shared Link?

    The shared link will create a long URL that you can copy and sent to another person. The person who opens this link will get to see your note as you have it saved.

    They will not be able to edit and resave your note (read-only). They can edit the note and then print it if they need to. Any updates you do on the note will be available immediately to the person you have shared the link with.

    The person you send the link to is not required to have an account with PryNotes.
