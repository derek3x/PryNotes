Help Section

Attach files to your notes. Documents, pictures, or archives.

Attach a file to your note that you can access later. These can be important documents that go with your note, or they can be as simple as pictures. Attaching a file will add a link on your note that will download your file anywhere, anytime.

Share your notes. Send anyone a link to your notes

You can create a shared URL link to your note that you can send to anyone. They can then see and print your note with no need to have a PryNotes account.

No computer? No Problem.

PryNotes.com will work on most newer smart phones and tablets. If you have any problems you can send us an issue report through our GitHub page.
